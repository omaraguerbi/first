Biography

Kendrick Lamar Duckworth is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer. He was born on June 17, 1987 in Compton, California. Lamar began his music career as a teenager under the stage name K-Dot, releasing a mixtape that garnered local attention. He later signed to Top Dawg Entertainment and released his debut album, Section.80, in 2011. Lamar has since released several critically acclaimed albums, including good kid, m.A.A.d city (2012), To Pimp a Butterfly (2015), DAMN. (2017), and Black Panther: The Album (2018). He has won numerous awards for his music, including 13 Grammy Awards.
